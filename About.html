OUR STORY

Hello there! This is Bims cook your No.1 food court in Nigeria.
We offer delicious African and Intercontinental dishes with class which will make your taste buds want more.
Bims cook started as an outdoor catering service in 1997 before the flagship restaurant was established in 2002 at plot 13 king George v road, Ikoyi.
We now have our franchise in all the major cities in Nigeria to offer you our delicious meals and excellent service which is the core value of our brand.
We are now here to serve you better with our Home delivery service which was recently added as such you can now order from our website from the comfort your home and expect your orders delivered at your door step.
We are always here to serve you better!
